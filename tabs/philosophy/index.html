    history

    bcrawl is a fork of DCSS. It started in 2018 as a continuation of the X-Crawl fork.

    test test

    notable differences from DCSS

    bcrawl has:

    • different monsters, player species, and backgrounds
    • different spells and more magic schools
    • many reworked deities
    • an extra branch (the Spriggan Forest)
    • no attacks of opportunity when monsters chase the player
    • food, and no Zot clock
    • a mode (Adventure Mode) where the player has 3 lives

    bcrawl has no trap effects on exploration. Instead, taking stairs can sometimes teleport the player. (There’s a special message when this could have happened.)
